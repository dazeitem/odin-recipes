My Prickle Brine recipe

Description

  • 1/2 Cup Vinegar
  • 1/2 Cup Water
  • 1 tbsp Sugar
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Allspice
  • 1/2 tsp Peppercorns
  • 1/2 tsp Black Mustard Seeds
  • 1 Bayleaf

  1. Put jars in a pot with cold water and let it boil.
  2. Add above ingredients to another pot.
  3. While waiting for the brine to boil, remove hot jars from boiling water.
  4. Fill the jars with cut pickling veggies.
  5. Add boiling brine to veggies and immediately close with lid.
