Mom's Omlette Ricetta

Description

  • 2 Eggs (whipped, room temp)
  • 2 Parsley stems w/ leaves, chopped
  • 1/4 of an Onion, chopped
  • Grated cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil

  1. Add oil to very hot pan and wait until oil is hot.
  2. Add eggs and spread around until pan is covered.
  3. Flip eggs when just browned.
  4. Add onions, parsley and cheese to middle of the omlette.
  5. Close the omlette and immediately place onto serving plate.
