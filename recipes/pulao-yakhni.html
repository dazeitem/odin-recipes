Yakhni Pulao

Description

  • Cooked rice (just cooked)
  • Chicken pieces
  • Gharam masala
  • Onion
  • 2 tbsp butter/Ghee
  • Yogurt
  • Garlic and ginger paste
  • Potatoes
  • Curry spice
  • Extra whole spices

  1. Add whole spices to a hot pan and roast until aromatic.
  2. Add butter, chopped onion and cook until translucent.
  3. Add garlic and ginger paste and fine spices to yogurt and coat chicken.
  4. Add chicken to pot, pour a small amount of water into pan.
  5. Turn heat to medium and let simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Add potatoes and cook until soft.
  7. Pour everything into baking dish and add rice. Mix well.
  8. Cover dish with foil and place in preheated 180 deg C oven.
  9. Cook until rice is just moist, but not dry.
